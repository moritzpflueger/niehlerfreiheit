Title: Association statutes

----

Text: The 'Kalker Freiheit' is an urban cultural association with a directly charitable purpose. The aim of the association is to shape urban living space together and to use it culturally in the long term. In order to create a place of interdisciplinary and social exchange for outsiders, cultural events of all kinds are held on the site.
